Here are some of my favorite restaurants, one located back at my hometown in the island of Tinian, a place where I grew up and it's wonderful homey cuisines and another favorite located where I currently reside in Garapan,Saipan.   2. Dolphin Restaurant–Location: Garapan, Saipan–Top floor of Hafa Adai' Beach Hotel

JC Cafè Restaurant

About

Located in the beautiful island of Tinian, JC Cafè is a well-known and family oriented restaurant where everyone is welcome to order! Their unqiue combinations of Filipino, Chamorro cuisine has stolen hearts of many and have become a favorite place to visit for both locals and visitors!

Dining

JC Cafè offers and serves a variety of cuisines, a mixture of Filipino and Chamorro cuisines, it's become a local favorite for everyone in the island and they are most known for their famous Buttered Chicken and for the locals, the Spicy Buttered Chicken.

The following are fan favorites oftenly ordered by locals and are recommended to try:

  • JC Burger with fries
  • Spicy Crispy Chicken Jalapeño Burger with fries
  • Spicy or Regular Buttered Chicken with homemade gravy
  • Sizziling Sisig
  • Tapsilog - a Filipino dish made with marinated beef, eggs, and garlic fried rice.

Restaurant Information

JC Cafè is a place that is a must try for all those who visit Tinian. Amazing food, friendly staff, and a welcoming atmosphere once you enter those doors.

If you're interested in placing an order, or if you're interested in joining the JC family, you may contact them at 1-670-433-4413.

Dolphin Restaurant

About

Located in the beautiful island of Saipan, Dolphin Restaurant is the place to be if you are looking for something with fine dining and a delicous set of cusine which range from a mixture of local, and Asian cuisines. They offer lunch and dinner sets, and are seasonally changed. A perfect place for a family dinner, or for a date night with your special person.

Dining

Dolphin restaurant has a variety of meals, and set meals to offer. They serve local and Japanese cuisine, and sometimes a mixture of them both! They offer lunch and dinner sets every day on their Facebook page. Their meal sets include:

  • Soup of the day or Miso soup
  • Rice
  • Salad
  • Free bottomless iced tea or water

One of my favorites when visiting the restaurant is the view it offers. The interior decor of the restaurant provides a laid–back allure of the view of the ocean. One of the best parts is the almost 360° wide windows that surround the restaurant, characterized by light, and the wooden texture that provide you with that island fine dining feeling.

Restaurant Information

Dolphin restaurant is available from MONDAY–SUNDAY starting their lunch hours at: 11:30am–2:00pm, and their dinner hours start from, 5:30pm–9:00pm.

If you are interested in making a reservation or inquiries you may contact them at:1-670-234-6495.
